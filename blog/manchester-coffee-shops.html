The Coffee Shops I Actually Go to (And What I Wear to Them)

Manchester cafes lifestyle

I've always thought that the coffee shop you go to says a lot about your vibe. Are you a trendy, independent-type, or a more mainstream, chain-loyal kind of person? For me, it's all about the atmosphere – I love going to coffee shops that feel like a reflection of my own personal style.

In Manchester, I've got a few go-to spots that I just can't get enough of. Foundation Coffee House on Oxford Road is one of my favorites – it's always bustling, and the coffee is amazing. But it's also a bit of a polished crowd, if you know what I mean – I always feel like I need to dress up a bit when I go there. I tend to wear my more put-together outfits, complete with a blazer and a pair of heeled ankle boots. It's not exactly my everyday look, but it feels right for the atmosphere.

Fig + Sparrow in Chorlton is a completely different story. It's one of my favorite spots to relax and unwind, and I always feel like I can be myself there. I tend to wear something soft and vintage, usually with florals – it's just a really laid-back, easy-going vibe. I love going there on a Sunday morning, when it's quiet and peaceful, and I can just sit and read a book with a cup of coffee.

And then there's Bunny Jackson's – it's a bit of a Manchester institution, and I adore it. I always wear my leather jacket when I go there – it's just the coolest, most laid-back spot, and I feel like I can get away with wearing whatever I want. The atmosphere is always lively, and the coffee is amazing – it's just a great place to people-watch and soak up the energy.

I've been wanting to try out Elnecot for ages – I've heard great things about it, and I love the look of the interior. I'm not sure what I'll wear when I finally get around to going – maybe something a bit more experimental, just to see how it feels. But that's the thing about coffee shops, right? They're a reflection of our personalities, and the way we present ourselves to the world. So, which coffee shop do you go to, and what do you wear when you're there? I'm curious to know!
